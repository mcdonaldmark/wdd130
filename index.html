Utah

Utah is home of the famous rivalry between Brigham Young University and the University of Utah. The rivalry is intense and as such, has been dubbed as "The Holy War". As two of the biggest colleges in Utah, the football matchup between the two schools are met with a large population of fans who want nothing better than their respective teams to win.
